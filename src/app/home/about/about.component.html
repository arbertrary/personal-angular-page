About Me

I am employed at GEOMAR Helmholtz Centre for Ocean Research Kiel through MarDATA Helmholtz School for Marine Data Science

My PhD project at GEOMAR focusses on the topic of provenance of immersive visualization workflows.

I am working with the Unreal Engine to create immersive tools for interactive data visualization, both in Head-Mounted Display VR as well as spatially immersive environments.

This also led me to gain experience with Virtual Production using Unreal Engine, nDisplay, and multi-projection technology.

Previously my work- and education-related interest and experience has been mostly in functional programming and natural language processing.

  • The incredible Worldbuilding of Brandon Sanderson's Cosmere
  • Bouldering and why all Programmers seem to love it
  • Why there are so many different guitars and what you can do with them!
